1\. Scope of Application

- (a) Personal registration information you provide when registering for this App account as required by this App;
- (b) Information automatically received and recorded by this App from your browser and computer when you use this App's network services or visit this App's platform webpage, including but not limited to your IP address, browser type, language used, access date and time, software and hardware feature information, and webpage records you requested;
- (c) User personal data obtained by this App through legal means from business partners.

You understand and agree that the following information is not subject to this Privacy Policy:

- (a) Keyword information you input when using the search services provided by this App platform;
- (b) Information data collected by this App regarding your posts on this App, including but not limited to participation in activities, transaction information, and evaluation details;
- (c) Acts violating laws or this App's rules and measures this App has taken against you.


2\. Information Use

(a)Permission Explanation

Location: To help users view their own location and share it with group members

Camera: To facilitate users sending pictures and video messages

SMS: To help users send SMS to contacts and verify verification codes

Phone: To enable users to make calls to contacts

Storage: To store messages for users to review and replay

Microphone: To record voice messages for walkie-talkie function with group users

(b)This App will not use or provide users' contact list, GPS, recording and other information to any unrelated third party.

(c)The camera permission of this App can be enabled or disabled by users. When enabled, it is only used for taking photos within this App for editing avatars and sending pictures. The microphone permission can be enabled or disabled by users. When enabled, it is only used for voice recording within this App for walkie-talkie function. This App will not collect, sell or disseminate users' relevant photos and voice information.

(d)This App will not provide, sell, rent, share or trade your personal information to any unrelated third party without your prior permission, except when such third party and this App (including this App's affiliated companies) individually or jointly provide services to you, and after the service ends, they will be prohibited from accessing all such information they were previously able to access.

(e)This App also does not permit any third party to collect, edit, sell or disseminate your personal information by any means. Any user of this App platform found engaging in the above activities will have their service agreement terminated immediately by this App.

Under the following circumstances, this App will disclose your personal information in whole or in part according to your personal wishes or legal requirements:

(a) Disclosure to third parties with your prior consent (including but not limited to: your confirmation of consent through electronic means);

(b)Sharing your personal information with third parties necessary to provide the products and services you requested, with your prior consent (including but limited to: your confirmation of consent through electronic means);

(a) All accounts of this App have security protection features. Please keep your username and password information properly. Notwithstanding the aforementioned security measures, please note that there is no "perfect security measure" on information networks. You should still take effective self-protection measures such as changing your login password regularly.

(b) When using this App's network services for online transactions, you will inevitably need to disclose your personal information (such as contact details or postal address) to the counterparty or potential counterparty. Please protect your personal information properly and only provide it to others when necessary. If you find that your personal information has been disclosed, especially if your username and password for this App have been compromised, please contact our customer service immediately. You may also report to the police so that this App can take appropriate measures.
