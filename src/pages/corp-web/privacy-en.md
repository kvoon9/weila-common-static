This website will use and disclose your personal information in accordance with the provisions of this Privacy Policy. However, this website will treat this information with a high degree of diligence and prudence. Unless otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, this website will not disclose or provide this information to third parties without your prior permission. This website will update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When you agree to this website's Terms of Service, you are deemed to have agreed to all contents of this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy is an integral part of this website's Terms of Service.

1\. Scope of Application

- (a) Personal registration information you provide when registering for this website account according to this website's requirements;
- (b) Information automatically received and recorded by this website from your browser and computer when you use this website's network services or visit this website's platform pages, including but not limited to your IP address, browser type, language used, access date and time, software and hardware characteristic information, and webpage records you requested;
- (c) User personal data obtained by this website through legal means from business partners.

You understand and agree that the following information does not apply to this Privacy Policy:

- (a) Keyword information you enter when using search services provided by this website platform;
- (b) Information data collected by this website regarding your publications on this website, including but not limited to participation in activities, transaction information, and evaluation details;
- (c) Actions that violate laws or this website's rules and measures this website has taken against you.

2\. Information Use

(a) This website will not provide, sell, rent, share, or trade your personal information to any unrelated third party, unless prior permission is obtained from you, or that third party and this website (including this website's affiliated companies) individually or jointly provide services to you, and after that service ends, they will be prohibited from accessing all such materials including those they could previously access.

(b) This website also does not permit any third party to collect, edit, sell, or disseminate your personal information free of charge by any means. Any user of this website platform found engaging in the above activities will have their service agreement immediately terminated by this website.

3\. Information Disclosure

Under the following circumstances, this website will disclose your personal information in whole or in part according to your personal wishes or legal provisions:

(a) Disclosure to third parties after obtaining your prior consent (including but not limited to: your consent confirmation via electronic data);

(b) To provide products and services you request, after obtaining your prior consent (including but not limited to: your consent confirmation via electronic data), it is necessary to share your personal information with third parties;

(c) According to relevant legal provisions, or at the request of supervisory, administrative, or judicial authorities, disclose to supervisory, administrative, or judicial authorities in accordance with the law;

(d) If you are a qualified intellectual property complainant and have filed a complaint, at the request of the complained party, disclose your intellectual property related information (including but not limited to: intellectual property number, intellectual property form, intellectual property owner, etc.) to facilitate resolution of possible rights disputes; if you are unwilling to disclose, you must inform this company when filing the complaint, otherwise it will be deemed that you agree to disclosure.

(e) In a transaction created on this website platform, if any party to the transaction performs or partially performs transaction obligations and requests information disclosure, this website has the right to decide to provide that user with necessary information such as the name and contact information of their transaction counterparty to facilitate completion of the transaction or resolution of disputes.

(f) Other disclosures this website deems appropriate according to laws, regulations, or website policies.

4\. Information Storage and Exchange

Information and materials about you collected by this website will be stored on servers of this website and/or its affiliated companies. This information and materials may be transmitted to countries, regions, or locations outside where this website collects information and materials, and may be accessed, stored, and displayed overseas.

5\. Information Security

(a) This website accounts have security protection functions. Please properly keep your username and password information. This website will ensure your information is not lost, misused, or altered through security measures such as encrypting user passwords. Despite the aforementioned security measures, please also note that "perfect security measures" do not exist on information networks. You should still take effective self-protection measures such as regularly changing login passwords.

(b) When using this website's network services for online transactions, you will inevitably disclose your personal information to transaction counterparties or potential transaction counterparties, such as contact information or postal address. Please properly protect your personal information and only provide it to others when necessary. If you discover your personal information has been leaked, especially if your website username and password have been compromised, please immediately contact this website's customer service, and you may also contact the police, so this website can take appropriate measures.

6\. Changes to This Privacy Policy

(a) If we decide to change the privacy policy, we will publish these changes in this policy, on our company website, and in locations we deem appropriate, so you can understand how we collect and use your personal information, who can access this information, and under what circumstances we will disclose this information.

(b) To ensure this policy complies with Chinese law (excluding Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau regions), our company reserves the right to modify this policy at any time, so please check frequently. If significant changes are made to this policy, our company will notify through website announcements.
